DEFINITION OF PARISHIONER:

Tuition is based on your status as a member of the Resurrection Parish. A Parishioner is an individual or a family who has been registered in Resurrection Church, regularly attends services at Resurrection Church and has been using the envelope system on a continuing basis.

(Minimum envelope contributions should total at least the difference between Parishioner and Non-Parishioner tuition.) Please use the table above as a guideline and please note that this is the minimum contribution in addition to your regular Church donation.

Contribution To Scholarship Program

You may elect to provide money to the school, in addition to the required tuition, which will be used towards the Scholarship Program. You may contribute to the Scholarship program by sending your donation to the Resurrection School Foundation at the School Office.
