  • We strongly prefer that children entering our Montessori program be years old by September 1st. However, we will accept children at or beyond this date if space is available. For any child accepted whose birth date is after September 1st, and who is experiencing academic or social adjustment difficulties, a conference will be held with the parents to discuss the continuation of the child in our Montessori Program.

For Those Entering Kindergarten, New York State Law Requires:

  1. Every child must have a physical examination upon entrance to kindergarten, 2nd, 4th, and 7th grades or enterance to a new school. The physical must be completed prior to entry.
  2. All children must be immunized against: measles (2 doses), mumps, rubella, diptheria, polio, and Hepatits B (3 doses) and varivax.

Proof of physical examination and immunization must be received BEFORE your child enters our school. No child wil be admitted without complete health forms.
